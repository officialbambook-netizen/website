FAQ

The questions worth answering before you try them.

Bambook compression gloves are a comfort and support product. For medical advice, a diagnosed condition, or symptoms that worry you, speak with a qualified professional.

When should I wear MyBambook?

Use them during everyday moments when your hands want support but you still need your fingers free: typing, reading, chores, errands, or quiet evening rest.

Can I type or use my phone with them on?

Yes. The fingerless design keeps your fingertips free so everyday touch tasks stay practical.

How should the compression feel?

They should feel close and supportive, not sharp, numb, or restrictive. If a glove pinches or feels uncomfortable, choose a different size.

What makes MyBambook different?

A real size guide instead of a guess, a straightforward 60-day guarantee instead of fine print, and gentle bamboo fabric built for actual daily wear — not just how it photographs.

Is this medical treatment?

No. Bambook compression gloves are a comfort and support product. They are not medical treatment and do not replace professional medical advice.

Can I order now?

Yes. Choose your size, pick 1 pair (₪179) or 2 pairs (₪219 — the bundle most people choose), and add to cart. Every order is covered by our 60-day money-back guarantee.
