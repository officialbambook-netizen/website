Quick answers.

How do I swap the filter cartridge?

Unscrew the old cartridge from the bottom of your Lavero shower head, drop the new one in, and screw it back. About 30 seconds — no tools, no plumber. A simple swap guide gets emailed with every order if you'd like a refresher.

How long does each filter last?

Each cartridge lasts approximately 90 days with regular daily use. Stocking up on a bundle covers 6 or 9 months at a stretch so you don't have to think about it. Choose Single, 2-pack, or Buy 2 Get 1 in the buy section above.

What makes Lavero different from other shower filters?

Lavero is designed specifically for beauty — hair, skin, and the products you already use. It reduces up to 99% of chlorine and creates a cleaner-feeling rinse that works with your existing routine, not around it. It's not a whole-home water softener; it's a precision beauty tool for your shower.

What if I don't love it?
