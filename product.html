FAQ

Quick answers.

When should I wear MyBambook?

Use them during the everyday moments when your hands want support but you still need your fingers free: typing, reading, chores, errands, or quiet evening rest.

Can I type or use my phone with them on?

Yes. The fingerless design keeps your fingertips free so everyday touch tasks stay practical.

How should the compression feel?

They should feel close and supportive, not sharp, numb, or restrictive. If a glove pinches or feels uncomfortable, choose a different size.

What makes MyBambook different?

A real size guide instead of a guess, a straightforward 60-day guarantee instead of fine print, and gentle bamboo fabric built for actual daily wear — not just how it photographs.

Is this medical treatment?

No. Bambook compression gloves are a comfort and support product. For medical advice or a diagnosed condition, speak with a qualified professional.
